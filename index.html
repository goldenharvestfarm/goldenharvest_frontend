About Golden Harvest Farm

Welcome to Golden Harvest Farm, your trusted source for quality livestock in Rwanda. We specialize in raising and selling healthy chickens and goats for consumption.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, healthy, and affordable livestock to our community while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and customer service.

Our Values

  • Quality: We ensure all our livestock are healthy, vaccinated, and well-cared for.
  • Transparency: Fair pricing and honest dealings with all our customers.
  • Sustainability: We practice responsible farming methods that respect the environment.
  • Community: Supporting local food security and economic growth.

What We Offer

  • High-quality chickens (various breeds including broilers, layers, and local breeds)
  • Healthy goats (meat breeds including Boer, Alpine, and local varieties)
  • Health certificates for all livestock
  • Delivery options available
  • Expert advice on livestock care

Our Location

We are based in Kigali, Rwanda, and serve customers throughout the country. Contact us to arrange a visit to our farm or to discuss your livestock needs.
