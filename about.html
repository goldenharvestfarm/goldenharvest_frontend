WHO WE ARE

A Family Farm Built on Trust & Quality

Welcome to Golden Harvest Farm, your trusted source for quality livestock in Rwanda. We specialize in raising and selling healthy chickens and goats for consumption — from our land directly to your hands, with no middlemen.

Every animal on our farm is raised with care, proper nutrition, and regular health checks. We believe that good food starts with healthy, well-treated animals.
